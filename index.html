Welcome to MessageSwiper.

This page will eventually hold links to a blog post or two about the development of MessageSwiper, starting from THEOS installation and ending with submission to BigBoss. Hopefully it will be of help to future developers faced with the same obstacles as I.

If you'd like to download MessageSwiper on Cydia, you can find the tested and proven version on the BigBoss repo. I upload beta builds for beta tester to my own repo at cydia.myrepospace.com/MattCMultimedia. You can also view the page here.
